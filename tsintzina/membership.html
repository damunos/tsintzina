Existing Members

We encourage you and your families to register and pay dues as official members of the Tsintzinian Society. Current dues are as follows:

  • Individual: $50

  • Family: $80

  • Benefactor: $120

Pay member dues
 
 
 
 

New Members

Becoming a member shows your support for educating future generations of their ancestral ties to Greece. We hope that you join our tight-knit, friendly community that has been meeting annually in Jamestown, NY since 1895!

BECOME A MEMBER
 

New Members:

To pay online, go to the Membership page, complete the new member form under "New Member", "Become a Member". If you do not see the payment screen when you submit, please go back to Membership page and choose "Pay Member Dues" under "Existing Members". Follow the instructions; choose "Pay with PayPal" NOTE: Site accepts credit cards, although the screen will say PayPal. When you get to the payment screen, scroll down to see "add debit or credit card".

Existing Members:

To pay online, go to the Membership page, click onto "Pay Member Dues" under "Existing Members". Follow the instructions; choose "Pay with PayPal" NOTE: Site accepts credit cards, although the screen will say PayPal. When you get to the payment screen, scroll down to see "add debit or credit card.

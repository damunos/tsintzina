Tsintzina Society: the oldest Greek heritage society in the United States

Come celebrate and learn about our cultural ties to the Greek mountain village of Tsintzina and surrounding area.

All are welcome!

 
Become a Member
 
 

The Tsintzina Society is a Greek heritage society based in Jamestown, NY. Since 1895, members have met each summer at the society's club just off the shores of Lake Chautauqua to commemorate our ancestral ties to the mountain villages of Tsintzina, Zoupena, and Goritsa in the southern Peloponnese region of Greece. Along with our friends and families of all backgrounds, we encourage you to visit our History page to learn more about the Tsintzina society and welcome you to attend our Annual Convention, held annually on the last weekend in July.
